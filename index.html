About Us

Welcome to SkillLumen, your gateway to industry-relevant internship experiences! Founded in 2024, we are a startup dedicated to bridging the gap between academic learning and industry requirements. We provide students with hands-on exposure to real-world projects and industry-standard tools.

Our mission is to equip students with practical skills and experience that prepare them for the professional world. Through our carefully designed internship programs, students gain valuable insights into their chosen fields while working on meaningful projects.
